About this project

Bachelor of Technology and Livelihood Education major in Home Economics graduates have a wide range of work opportunities in the field of culinary arts, nutrition, food service management, and event planning. They can pursue careers as bank teller, teacher, front desk specialist, or start their own catering business. With the increasing demand for healthy eating and lifestyle choices, there is a growing need for professionals who can educate and assist individuals and communities in making informed decisions about food and nutrition.
